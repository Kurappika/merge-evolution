## Description

Merge Evolution is a puzzle game where players place tiles on a grid. When three or more identical tiles are connected orthogonally (up, down, left, or right), they merge into a single, upgraded tile at the position where the last tile was placed. The goal is to plan your moves carefully to create massive chain reactions, achieve the highest-level tile possible, and get the highest score before the board fills up!

## How to Play

1.  **Select Your Setup:** Choose a **Board Size** and a **Theme** from the control panel on the left.
2.  **Start the Game:** Click the **"New Game"** button.
3.  **Place Your Tile:** The **"Next Tile"** is shown in the control panel. Click on any empty square on the board to place it.
4.  **Merge Tiles:** If your placed tile connects with two or more identical tiles, they will all merge into a single tile of the next level.
5.  **Score Points:** Merging tiles increases your score. Longer chains and higher-level merges give you more points.
6.  **Game Over:** The game ends when the board is completely full and no more moves can be made.
7.  **Play Again:** Click **"Play Again"** on the game over screen to start a new challenge!
